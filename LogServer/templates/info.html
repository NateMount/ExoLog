What Is ExoLog?

Exolog is a modern logging solution platform for distributed and concurent systems. We support both large scale and personal projects. With a variety of applications across multiple languages, ExoLog holds support for a variety of logging mediums to track your applications progress and development

How Does it Work?

ExoLog works to be as lightweight and reliable as possible
